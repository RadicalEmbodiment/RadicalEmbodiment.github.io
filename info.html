PRACTICAL INFORMATION


How to get to Murcia?

From Europe: The best option is to fly to the Alicante Airport (ALC) or the Murcia Airport (RMU) and then getting to the city via bus or train. The commute from ALC is ∼45 minutes; the commute from RMU is ∼25 minutes.

From Outside Europe: Getting to the Alicante Airport (ALC) is still the best option. If not possible, the best option is to fly to Madrid and to take a train from Madrid to Murcia (∼3 hours).

If you travel to the Alicante Airport (ALC)...

You can get a bus to Murcia in the airport in this webpage: Buses ALSA

You can get a short trip on the city bus (C6) to Alicante. The Train Station stop is "Estación-Salamanca". Then, you can take a train from Alicante to Murcia. You can find the tickets in this webpage: RENFE

General information about the Alicante Airport (ALC) and transportation here: Alicante Airport

From Spain: Multiple options via flights, trains, and road.
